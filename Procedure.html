Preparation

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until a smooth dough forms.
  4. On a floured surface, roll out the dough to about 1/2 inch thickness.
  5. Use a donut cutter or two differently sized circular cutters to cut out donut shapes with holes in the center.
  6. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  7. Carefully add a few donuts to the hot oil, frying each side for about 1-2 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Remove the fried donuts with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil.
  9. While the donuts are still warm, roll them in the remaining 1/2 cup of granulated sugar to coat them evenly.
  10. Enjoy the delicious sugar donuts as they are or with your favorite glaze, icing, or toppings!
